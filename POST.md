Leadership determines layoffs, not technology.

#AI

---

**About this build:** It is intentionally small (canvas + ES modules, procedural audio, no binary assets) so it stays easy to host and fork. The “cost AI subsides” here is not polish for its own sake—it is the time you reclaim to spend on research, context, audience, and what actually ships.

**Mobile:** The game letterboxes to **16:9** inside the browser. **Landscape** shows more of the track horizontally; **portrait** works but is narrower—use whichever you prefer. On-screen controls appear for coarse pointers or narrower viewports.
